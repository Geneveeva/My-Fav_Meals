Ofe owerri

This food is popularly eaten in the Eatern part of Nigeria, precisely Imo state.

Ingredients Measurements
ugu 2 bowls
maggi 2 cubes
    How to prepare
  • Start by boiling your ede first.
  • In another pot, boil your assorted meat, stockfish and dry fish, three cubes of seasoning and onions
  • Then add a generous amount of water afterwards will make up the quantity of the soup.
  • While the meat heats, take out your ede which should be soft, and pound.
  • Taste to know if any ingredient is not represented in the food, then add your ugu.
  • Allow boil for a minute, turn off the gas and food is ready.
  • Serve with whatever swallow of your choice.

Egusi soup

This soup is eaten by both the Eastern, Southern and Western people of Nigeria

Ingredients Measurements
Melon 2 bowls
salt 1 tbs
    How to prepare
  • Add ground melon to a bowl, add about a cup of water and mix to form a paste then set aside.
  • Place a big pan on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes
  • Add pepper mix, locust beans and stir to combine.
  • Bring to boil for 5 minutes.
  • Add the egusi paste in bits to pepper
  • Add smoked turkey,crayfish, beef and stir to combine.
  • Serve and enjoy with any Nigerian swallow of choice

Fried Yam

Yam is root crop popularly eaten by many

Ingredients Measurements
1 tuber of yam 3 cups of water
salt 1 tbs
    How to prepare
  • Peel the bark of yam
  • add to hot oil
  • fry for few minutes
  • Serve and Enjoy!
